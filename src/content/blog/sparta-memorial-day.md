**A fitting remembrance.** On Memorial Monday, the VFW, Marine League, and American Legion called on citizens to gather at **Veterans Memorial Park** in Sparta to remember and honor the sacrifice of those who died in military service.

Officer of the Day David Taylor placed a red flower on the memorial wreath, and Patty Harris, N.C. VFW Auxiliary President, spoke to the approximately **120 neighbors in attendance**. Seated in the front row — and introduced to the crowd — were Korean War and World War II veterans Bob Robertson, Russell Edwards, Luther Marlowe, and Mack Osborne.

That's Memorial Day in a town like Sparta: before the cookouts and the first river trips of summer, the flags line Main Street and the whole county shows up to remember its own.
