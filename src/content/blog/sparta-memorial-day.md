Memorial Day weekend in the mountains means cookouts, the first river trips of the season, and porch evenings that stretch late. But in a town like Sparta, it means something more first.

Alleghany County remembers its own. Before the grills light up, flags line Main Street and neighbors gather to honor the servicemen and women who never came home.

From all of us at Greenfield Acres Realty: enjoy the weekend, hug your people, and take a moment for the reason we have it.
