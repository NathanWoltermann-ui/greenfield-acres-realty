When national publications rank the best Southern mountain towns to live in — not just visit — the North Carolina High Country keeps showing up.

The reasons are the ones retirees and remote workers tell us every week: four mild seasons, a lower cost of living than comparable mountain destinations, real small-town community, and the Blue Ridge Parkway as your scenic commute.

Towns like Blowing Rock, Boone, West Jefferson, and Sparta each offer a different flavor of mountain living, from storybook village to college-town energy to wide-open farm country.
