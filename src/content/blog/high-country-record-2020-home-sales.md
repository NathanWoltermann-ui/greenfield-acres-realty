More area homes were sold in 2020 than during **any other year on record**. The High Country Association of REALTORS® end-of-year market highlights:

| | 2019 | 2020 | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Residential home sales** | 2,608 | 3,253 | **+24.7%** |
| **Total residential sales value** | $804.7M | **$1.182B** | **+46.9%** |
| **Land sales** | 656 | 1,145 | **+74.5%** |

Sales grew in every county the MLS covers:

- **Alleghany**: 176 → 250
- **Ashe**: 444 → 541
- **Avery**: 551 → 712
- **Watauga**: 1,224 → 1,428

The story behind the numbers: buyers seeking space, views, and small-town safety discovered the High Country in record numbers — many relocating full-time now that remote work made it possible. And note that land sales grew fastest of all: the build-your-own-mountain-home wave was just getting started.
