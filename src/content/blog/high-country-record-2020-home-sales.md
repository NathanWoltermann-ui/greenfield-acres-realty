More area homes were sold in 2020 than during **any other year on record**, according to the High Country Association of REALTORS®.

The pandemic accelerated a trend we'd been watching for years: buyers seeking space, views, and small-town safety discovered the North Carolina High Country in record numbers — many of them relocating full-time now that remote work made it possible.
