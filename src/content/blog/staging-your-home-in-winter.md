Many people believe winter is an unfavorable time to sell a home. In the High Country, the opposite can be true: winter buyers are serious buyers, and a mountain home glowing warm against the snow is its own best advertisement.

A few things that work in your favor this season:

- **Serious traffic.** Nobody browses homes in January for fun — winter showings tend to be motivated buyers on a timeline.
- **Cozy sells.** A lit fireplace, warm lighting, and simple seasonal touches let buyers feel what winter in the mountains is supposed to feel like.
- **Less competition.** With fewer listings on the market, a well-presented home stands out.
