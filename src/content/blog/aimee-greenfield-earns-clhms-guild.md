*From the Institute for Luxury Home Marketing — Sparta, NC, October 25, 2022:*

Aimee Greenfield with Greenfield Acres Realty in Sparta, NC earned The Institute for Luxury Home Marketing's **Million Dollar GUILD™** recognition for experience, knowledge, and expertise in million-dollar and above residential properties. Greenfield is a Certified Luxury Home Marketing Specialist™ (CLHMS), part of an exclusive group of real estate professionals who have completed The Institute's training and have a proven performance in the upper-tier market.

> "Agents who have earned the GUILD™ recognition are performing at the highest level in their community. It takes a superior level of service to be successful and consistently close sales at or above one million dollars. We want to recognize this performance and give these elite professionals the earned distinction that they are the agent of choice for affluent buyers and sellers."
> — Diane Hartley, President of The Institute

"As both a Certified Luxury Home Marketing Specialist and GUILD Recognition awardee, you can be confident you're choosing an expert with the training, resources, and proven performance in buying and selling high-end homes when you work with me," said Greenfield.

Greenfield has been in real estate since 2015 and specializes in North Carolina's High Country — Blowing Rock, Banner Elk, Boone, West Jefferson and Sparta. Greenfield Acres Realty holds a continual 5-star rating while advertising using multimedia and the most up-to-date, effective marketing techniques available — turning over every stone possible for clients.
