Leaf season is here. According to NC State's College of Natural Resources, leaves can begin changing as early as **late September**, with colors peaking as early as **mid-October** at the High Country's elevations.

Our favorite viewing advice: skip the crowds at the famous overlooks and drive the Parkway between Doughton Park and Blowing Rock on a weekday morning — then find a porch and watch the mountainsides turn.
