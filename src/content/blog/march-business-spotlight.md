Greenfield Acres Realty was recognized by the Chamber of Commerce as its March Business Spotlight, honoring the firm's community involvement in Alleghany County.

> "Buying and selling a home or business is one of life's most important decisions. Clients choose us because they trust us! With integrity, we help buy and sell property at the best price the market will bear. With knowledge and efficiency, we offer our clients a positive experience with the least possible inconvenience."

Thank you to the Chamber and to the neighbors and clients who make Sparta such a special place to work.
