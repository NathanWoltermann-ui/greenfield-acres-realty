The housing market is booming. With plenty of jobs to choose from, residents from every background are making North Carolina their home.

U-Haul ranked North Carolina **third** in the nation for inbound moves, trailing only Florida and Texas — a considerable jump from 24th place in the previous year's report.

Plenty of those movers are discovering what we already know: the Blue Ridge Mountains offer some of the best living in the state.
