Rent or own? Here's how the two really stack up:

**Freedom.** Renting means landlord restrictions — right down to "no Fluffy allowed." Owning means no landlord, pets welcome, and the freedom to change what you want: paint any color you please. Bonus: buy a newer home and maintenance runs lower, with no old appliances to fix and better energy savings.

**Investment.** As a renter, as much as **40% of your income** is going to your landlord — and he's the one winning. As an owner, appreciating value builds *your* wealth, home equity becomes *your* capital, and your mortgage interest earns *you* a tax deduction. **78% of homebuyers say their house is the best investment they ever made.**

**Stability.** Who wants to play the "will I be evicted?" game every lease renewal? Owning means putting down roots and feeling part of the community — block party included.
