Winter is one of the High Country's best-kept secrets. When the snow flies, families here have choices most of the South drives hours for:

- **Downhill skiing & snowboarding** at Appalachian Ski Mtn., Beech Mountain, and Sugar Mountain
- **Snow tubing** at Hawksnest — one of the largest tubing parks on the East Coast
- **Ice skating** outdoors at Appalachian Ski Mtn. and Beech Mountain's Alpine Village
- **Winter hiking** on the Parkway's quieter trails, with leafless views that summer visitors never see
