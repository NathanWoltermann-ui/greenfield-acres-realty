The Blue Ridge Parkway was the **most visited National Park Service site in 2020**, welcoming more than **14.1 million visitors**.

For those of us who live along it, that's no surprise — America's favorite drive runs right through the High Country, with Milepost 234 minutes from our Sparta office.

Fourteen million people visited last year. A lucky few get to call it home.
