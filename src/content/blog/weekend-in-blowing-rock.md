Travel blogger **Tianna Gratta** of The Passport Chronicles stumbled onto our favorite village — here's how her visit began:

> "I never heard of this sleepy little mountain town. I had no idea what to expect as I drove through the mountains into Blowing Rock. It was immensely overcast and I could barely see the road when I arrived. Starving, I parked on the main street and strolled around in order to find lunch.
>
> The little town was so cute. I had come to check out a hotel, but it was such a great weekend getaway spot. It's perched on a mountain, so from anywhere on Main Street the views are stunning.
>
> It is a perfect place if you want to explore the outdoors for a weekend…"

Her verdict matches what our buyers discover: a weekend in Blowing Rock has a way of turning into a search for a permanent address.
