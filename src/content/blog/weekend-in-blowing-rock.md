Travel blog **The Passport Chronicles** spent a weekend in Blowing Rock and documented what makes the village such a beloved Blue Ridge destination — the storybook Main Street, Moses Cone Manor and its carriage trails, the namesake Blowing Rock overlook, and the kind of inns and restaurants that keep visitors coming back season after season.

Their verdict matches what our buyers discover: a weekend in Blowing Rock has a way of turning into a search for a permanent address.
