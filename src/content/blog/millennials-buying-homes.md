Why are millennials buying their first home? A SunTrust Mortgage survey (conducted by Harris Poll) found:

- **66%** — more living space
- **36%** — build equity
- **33%** — better space or yard **for the dog**
- **25%** — marriage
- **19%** — birth of a child

Read that again: **more people list their dogs than marriage or children** as a factor in the home-buying decision. It makes sense — a fenced yard is one thing a landlord can rarely offer.
