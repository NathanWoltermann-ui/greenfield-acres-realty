A third of millennials who bought their first home were primarily influenced by the need for more space — not for children, but for their **pets**.

Surveys show that for young buyers, a fenced yard and room for a dog now rank ahead of weddings and kids as reasons to stop renting. It makes sense: a yard is one thing a landlord can rarely offer.
