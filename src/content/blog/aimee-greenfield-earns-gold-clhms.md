Aimee Greenfield has earned the **Certified Luxury Home Marketing Specialist (CLHMS)** designation at the **Gold** level from the Institute for Luxury Home Marketing.

The CLHMS designation isn't earned in a classroom alone — it requires documented performance in the upper tier of the market, proving sustained success marketing and selling luxury properties.

It's recognition of what Greenfield Acres clients already experience: luxury-brokerage marketing with small-mountain-town heart.
