*From the Institute for Luxury Home Marketing — Sparta, NC, September 17, 2021:*

Aimee Greenfield with Greenfield Acres Realty in Sparta, NC earned The Institute for Luxury Home Marketing's **Certified Luxury Home Marketing Specialist™ (CLHMS)** designation in recognition of experience, knowledge, and expertise in high-end residential properties. Greenfield joins an exclusive group of real estate professionals who have completed The Institute's training and have a proven performance in the upper-tier market.

> "Agents who have earned the CLHMS™ designation are performing at the highest level in their community. The CLHMS™ seal is a symbol of distinction. Affluent buyers and sellers from around the world look for this symbol of luxury home knowledge and expertise when putting their trust in a real estate professional."
> — Diane Hartley, President of The Institute

The specialized training and ongoing membership with The Institute provide Greenfield with the knowledge and tools to better serve clients. "I will utilize my background and experience to make sure my clients have as pleasurable and exciting a process as possible," said Greenfield.

Greenfield has been in real estate since 2015 and specializes in North Carolina's High Country — Blowing Rock, Banner Elk, Boone, West Jefferson and Sparta. Greenfield Acres Realty holds a continual 5-star rating while advertising using multimedia and the most up-to-date, effective marketing techniques available — turning over every stone possible for clients.
